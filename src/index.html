Mental Health Made Simple

At Thrive Talk, we believe that therapy should be accessible and affordable to everyone - without any stigma attached.

Join our network and get access to hundreds of licensed health professionals dedicated to providing you with personalized care and support.

We've already helped thousands of people take control of their mental health and work towards nurturing themselves, and it's all 100% confidential.

Tell us about your concerns and we'll match you to a licensed therapist within minutes.

Getting started is easy. Start by filling out a questionnaire about your needs and preferences, and we'll automatically match you with a therapist who specializes in your concerns.

You'll be able to schedule sessions at times that work best for you, so you can focus on your wellbeing at your own pace.

Affordable

We understand that financial struggles can greatly impact mental health, and we're not here to make things worse. We accept several insurance providers and have affordable flat rates.

Convenient

Get the right support when you need it by scheduling your therapy sessions as little or as often as you want. Communicate with your therapist 24/7 from the comfort of your home.

Private

All of our therapists are licensed professionals who value and respect your privacy. Your conversations will never be shared with anyone and your records remain 100% confidential.

Effective

Therapy is an excellent way to improve your mental health and stability, which can lead to improvements in other areas of your life and the lives of those around you.
